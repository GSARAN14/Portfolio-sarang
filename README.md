## Customization Guide

### 1. **Add Your Profile Photo**
Replace `profile.png` with your actual photo:
- Recommended size: 200x200px
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Place in the portfolio directory

### 2. **Update Your Information**

- `Your Name` â†’ Your actual name
- `AI/ML Engineer | Deep Learning Specialist | Data Scientist` â†’ Your title
- Update the About section with your bio

### 3. **Update Your Skills**
In the Skills Section, replace the skill tags with your actual skills:
- ML Frameworks: TensorFlow, PyTorch, etc.
- Programming Languages: Python, R, SQL, etc.
- Data Tools: Pandas, Spark, Hadoop, etc.
- Specializations: Computer Vision, NLP, etc.

### 4. **Add Your Projects**
Replace the 4 example projects with your real projects:
- Update project names and descriptions
- Add relevant tech tags
- Update project links (currently placeholder links)
- Change project badge categories (AI/ML, NLP, Computer Vision, etc.)

### 5. **Update About Section**
- Add your professional bio
- Update your statistics (5+ Years, 20+ Projects, etc.)
- Customize the description

### 6. **Add Social Media Links**
Update the social links in the header and footer:
- Replace `#` with your actual URLs
- GitHub, LinkedIn, Twitter, Kaggle links

### 7. **Update Footer Information**
Customize the footer with your information and update copyright year if needed.
